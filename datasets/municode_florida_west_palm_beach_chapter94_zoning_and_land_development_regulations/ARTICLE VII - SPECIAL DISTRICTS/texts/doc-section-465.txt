Sec. 94-205. - Conservation (CON) district.
Development standards in the conservation (CON) district shall be as follows:
1. Minimum lot dimensions:
a. Lot area: one acre.
b. Lot width: 100 feet.
2. Minimum setbacks:
a. Front: 30 feet.
b. Side: 30 feet.
c. Corner: 30 feet.
d. Rear: 30 feet.
e. Waterfront/Lakefront: 30 feet.
f. Minimum building separation: 24 feet.
3. Maximum lot coverage by buildings: ten percent.
4. Maximum lot coverage by impervious surfaces: 25 percent.
5. Maximum building height: 35 feet.
6. Minimum lot area: five acres per dwelling unit.
