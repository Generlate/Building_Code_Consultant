Sec. 94-201. - General development standards.
a. Parking. On-site and off-site parking and loading facilities in special districts shall be provided pursuant to ARTICLE
XV of this chapter.
b. Landscape. Landscaping in special districts shall be provided pursuant to ARTICLE XIV of this chapter.
c. Signs. Signs in special districts shall be allowed pursuant to ARTICLE XIII of this chapter.
d. Development regulations. Regulations governing height, bulk, mass, and placement of structures within all special
districts are provided in Table VII-1.
