Sec. 94-3. - Legislative purpose and intent.
The purpose and intent of the zoning code is to assist implementation of the city's comprehensive plan. The zoning code
is adopted to assist in carrying out the desired general policies of the comprehensive plan for managing the use of land,
water, and resources within the city. The zoning code is and shall remain consistent with the comprehensive plan, as
mandated by the state's local government comprehensive planning act. The zoning code shall be amended as necessary
to assure consistency with the city's comprehensive plan.
