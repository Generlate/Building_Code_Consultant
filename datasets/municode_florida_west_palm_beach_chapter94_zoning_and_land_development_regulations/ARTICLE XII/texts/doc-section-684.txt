Sec. 94-377. - Threatened, endangered, and rare species.
If an environmental assessment reveals the presence of a threatened, endangered, or rare species on the project site and
a likely adverse impact on that species from the proposed land use, the city shall consult with the state fish and wildlife
conservation commission prior to the issuance of an approval for that use with respect to the need for and practical
options to protect such species.
