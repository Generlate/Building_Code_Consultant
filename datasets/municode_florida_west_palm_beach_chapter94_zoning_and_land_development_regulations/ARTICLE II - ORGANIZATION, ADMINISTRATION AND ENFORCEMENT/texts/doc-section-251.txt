Sec. 94-52. - Enforcement and penalties.
a. Generally. It shall be unlawful for any person or entity to violate the historic preservation provisions of this chapter.
Such violations shall be enforced and penalties imposed according to the provisions of chapter 26, articles II and III.
In addition to all other employees authorized by the Code to enforce the Code, the historic preservation planners
are hereby designated as employees who are duly authorized to issue notices of violations of the historic
preservation provisions of this chapter.
b. Enforcement of maintenance and repair provisions. Should the historic preservation board or the historic
preservation division determine that any property is endangered by lack of maintenance and repair, a request shall
be made to the appropriate officials or agencies of the city to require correction of such deficiencies under authority
of applicable laws and regulations.
(Ord. No. 3554-02, art. VIII, 7-22-2002)
