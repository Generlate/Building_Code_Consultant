Sec. 94-374. - Water wellfields.
Development in water wellfields shall conform to the requirements of the county's wellfield protection ordinance.
Conformance with the requirements of such ordinance shall constitute compliance with this provision and with the
provisions of the city's comprehensive plan which relate to the protection of water wellfields.
