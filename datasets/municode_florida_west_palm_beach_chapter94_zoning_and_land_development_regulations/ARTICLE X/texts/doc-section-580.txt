Sec. 94-303. - Accessory apartments permitted with extra requirements.
In the event a property owner can not meet any of the additional standards shown in subsection (b), such waiver shall
require approval by the zoning board of appeals as a Class B special use permit.
a. Additional application requirements.
1. Only permitted in a single-family high density (SF14) residential zoning district.
2. Minimum lot area: 6,000 square feet
b. Additional standards.
1. All accessory apartments must comply with the development standards for residential accessory structures.
2. Lot width: 60 feet
3. Maximum unit size: 800 square feet.
4. Setbacks: See subsection 94-305(b)(2)
5. Parking: One parking space shall be provided for the apartment and two for the principal structure on site.
6. Maximum height: Accessory apartments shall not exceed 25 feet in height.
