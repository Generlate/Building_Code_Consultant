Sec. 94-447. - Existing native noninvasive plant materials.
a. Consideration of existing native noninvasive plant material. Healthy native noninvasive plant material existing on a
site prior to development or redevelopment may be utilized in meeting the requirements of this section.
b. Preservation of existing native noninvasive plant material. Existing native noninvasive plant material shall be
preserved in accordance with the provisions of this section and subsection 94-448(e)(1).
