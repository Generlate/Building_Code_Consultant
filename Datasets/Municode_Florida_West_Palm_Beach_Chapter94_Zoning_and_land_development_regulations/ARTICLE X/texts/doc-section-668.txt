Sec. 94-338. - Abandonment.
Towers, antennas and their associated equipment compound shall be removed, at the owner's expense, within 180
calendar days of cessation of use or abandonment. An owner wishing to extend the time for removal or reactivation shall
submit an application stating the reason for such extension. The city may extend the time for removal or reactivation up to
60 additional calendar days upon a showing of good cause. Upon removal of the tower, antenna, and equipment
compound, the development area shall be returned to its natural state and topography and vegetated consistent with the
natural surroundings or consistent with the current uses of the surrounding or adjacent land at the time of removal.
