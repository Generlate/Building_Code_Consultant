Sec. 94-307. - Surface water management.
All development shall provide for adequate drainage and stormwater management. Compliance with the stormwater
management requirements of South Florida Water Management District F.A.C. 40E-4, shall constitute compliance with
this provision and with the provisions of the city's comprehensive plan which relate to the aquifer recharge areas.
Provided, however, that there shall be no new drainage outfalls into Lake Worth.
