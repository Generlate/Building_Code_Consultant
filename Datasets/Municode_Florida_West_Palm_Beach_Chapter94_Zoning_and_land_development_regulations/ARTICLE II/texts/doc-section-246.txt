Sec. 94-47. - Certified local government review.
The city commission is a certified local government (CLG) approved by the state department of state, division of historical
resources. The city commission as a CLG is required to participate in the state National Register of Historic Places
nomination process, be involved in the section 106 process, and is eligible to receive grants from the certified local
government section of the state department of state historical resources grants-in-aid program.
