Sec. 94-571. - Short title; authority and application.
a. Short title. This article shall be known as, and may be cited as, the "City of West Palm Beach Concurrency
Management Ordinance."
b. Authority. The city commission has the authority to adopt this article pursuant to Art. VIII, Sec. 2(b), Fla. Const., the
Charter of the city, F.S. § 166.01 et. seq., F.S. § 163.3161(8), F.S. § 163.3177(10)(h), F.S. § 163.3202(2)(g), and
F.A.C. 9J-5.
c. Application. This article shall apply to all development within the city, as the boundaries of the city may exist from
time to time.
(Code 1979, § 33-240)
