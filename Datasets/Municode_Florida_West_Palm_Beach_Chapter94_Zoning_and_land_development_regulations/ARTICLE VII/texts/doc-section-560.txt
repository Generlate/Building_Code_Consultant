Sec. 94-233. - Coleman Park recreation and open space (CP-ROS) development
characteristics and sub-district requirements.
a. Intent. The intent of the district is to provide regulations for public open spaces and facilities designed to serve the
needs of the surrounding residential community.
b. Building height. Building height shall not exceed 40 feet, including all habitable and mechanical space.
c. Building placement.
1. Minimum lot area: 5,000 square feet.
2. Minimum building separation: 15 feet.
3. Minimum lot width: 50 feet.
4. Maximum FAR: 0.20
5. Minimum setbacks:
a. Front: 25 feet.
b. Corner: 15 feet.
c. Side: 15 feet.
d. Rear: 15 feet.
e. Setbacks shall not be used for off-street parking or driveways.
d. Maximum lot coverage by buildings: 50 percent.
e. Maximum lot coverage by impermeable surface: 75 percent.
f. Parking. All parking shall be placed behind the building whenever possible. At least one standard parking space
shall be provided per 1,000 square feet. On-street parking adjacent to the property may be counted toward the
required parking.
